Last updated: 23rd December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how mTerminal2go (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our Virtual Terminal mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. About mTerminal2go

mTerminal2go is a Virtual Terminal mobile application that allows merchants and businesses to accept payments, manage transactions, and view payment history securely through their mobile devices.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you register or use the App:

Full name

Email address

Phone number

Account login credentials

Customer information entered during transactions (e.g., name, email, phone number)

To provide virtual terminal services, we may collect:

Transaction amounts

Transaction references

Payment status and timestamps

Partial payment identifiers (we do not store full card details)

Device type and operating system

Date and time of access

The App may request access to:

Internet connectivity (required to process transactions)

Notifications (for transaction alerts and important updates)

You can manage permissions from your device settings at any time.

We use the collected information to:

Create and manage user accounts

Process and display payment transactions

Provide virtual terminal payment services

Send transaction notifications and service-related updates

Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience

Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or security incidents

All payments made through mTerminal2go are processed using secure third-party payment gateways.

We do not store or process sensitive payment credentials such as card numbers, CVV, or PINs on our servers. These providers operate under their own privacy and security policies and comply with applicable industry standards, including PCI-DSS.
